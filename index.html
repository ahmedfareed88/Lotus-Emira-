Lotus Emira
  • Horsepower: 360 hp (2.0L turbocharged inline-4) or 400 hp (3.5L supercharged V6).
  • Torque: 430 Nm (2.0L turbocharged engine) or 420 Nm (3.5L V6 engine).
  • Transmission: 6-speed manual or 6-speed automatic (depending on engine choice).
  • 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph): 4.3 seconds (V6 version).
